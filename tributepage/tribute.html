Mahendra Singh Dhoni -beyond boundaries

M.S.Dhoni

Dhoni was born on 7 July 1981 in Ranchi,Jharkhand in a Hindu Rajput family to Pan Singh and Devaki Devi.His parents hailed from Lwali village in Uttar Pradesh and he was the youngest of three children.His family spells the surname as "Dhauni".The spelling "Dhoni" emerged due to a spelling mistake in his school certificates and, despite repeated attempts by his family, has never been rectified.

Captain Cool

MS Dhoni began his cricket career with the Commando Cricket Club and Central Coal Fields Limited, making his Ranji Trophy debut in 1999. His domestic success led to his India A selection and ODI debut in 2004. He captained India to victories in the 2007 World Twenty20, 2011 Cricket World Cup, and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy. Dhoni retired from international cricket in 2020, leaving a lasting legacy.

Thala For A Reason

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, acquired by Chennai Super Kings (CSK) for US$1.5 million in 2008, led the team to five IPL titles and ten finals. After a stint with Rising Pune Supergiants during CSK's suspension, he returned to CSK in 2018, winning more titles. Dhoni became the first player to play 200 T20 matches for CSK and holds the record for most IPL appearances. Ahead of the 2024 season, he handed over the captaincy to Ruturaj Gaikwad.
